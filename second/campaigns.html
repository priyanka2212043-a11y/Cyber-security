Recent Victories

Coal Plant Shutdown

After 18 months of sustained pressure including protests, legal challenges, and media campaigns, the Riverside coal plant will close permanently. This prevents 2.3 million tons of annual CO2 emissions.

Region: North America

Pipeline Cancelled

The controversial Mountain Pass pipeline project has been permanently cancelled following our community-led resistance campaign. Protected: 450 km of pristine wilderness.

Region: Europe

Plastic Ban Enacted

Following our two-year advocacy campaign, the city council has banned single-use plastics in food service. This victory sets a precedent for other cities.

Region: Asia-Pacific

Protected Reserve Expanded

Our legal team successfully challenged logging permits, resulting in a 40% expansion of the Greenwood Nature Reserve and protection for endangered species.

Region: South America

How Our Campaigns Work

1. Research

Our investigative team gathers evidence, analyzes data, and builds airtight cases against environmental crimes.

2. Mobilize

We build coalitions, recruit volunteers, and coordinate direct actions through our decentralized chapter network.

3. Act

Through protests, media campaigns, legal action, and civil disobedience, we force decision-makers to respond.
