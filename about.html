About Green Truth

A global movement built on transparency, direct action, and the urgent need for environmental justice.

Our Story

Green Truth was founded in 2019 by a coalition of environmental scientists, activists, and concerned citizens who recognized that conventional approaches to climate action were failing. Corporations continued to greenwash their destructive practices. Governments set targets decades in the future while our planet burned today.

We decided enough was enough. Green Truth emerged as a radical response to this crisis - a movement committed to exposing environmental crimes, demanding immediate action, and mobilizing people power to force change.

Today, we are 150,000+ members strong across 47 countries, operating as a decentralized network of local chapters unified by our core principles and urgent mission.

Our Core Principles

Tell the Truth

We demand that governments and institutions tell the truth about the climate and ecological emergency. No more lies. No more greenwashing. The science is clear.

Act Now

The time for gradual change has passed. We need immediate, decisive action to halt environmental destruction and transition to sustainable systems before it's too late.

Justice for All

Environmental justice is social justice. Those least responsible for the crisis suffer most. We fight for equity and protect frontline communities.

Decentralized Power

We operate through self-organizing local chapters. This structure ensures resilience, creativity, and democratic participation at all levels.

Non-Violent Action

We use non-violent direct action and civil disobedience to disrupt business-as-usual and force meaningful change. Our tactics are bold but peaceful.

Transparency

We practice what we preach. All our activities, funding, and decision-making processes are transparent and publicly accountable.

How We Operate

Local Chapters

Green Truth operates through autonomous local chapters that organize actions in their communities. Each chapter follows our core principles while tailoring strategies to local contexts. Anyone can start a chapter.

Working Groups

Specialized teams coordinate on key functions: Research & Investigation, Legal Support, Media & Communications, Technology, Training, and Fundraising. All working groups are volunteer-led.

Direct Action

Our tactics include peaceful protests, occupations, blockades, and creative disruptions designed to generate media attention and pressure decision-makers. All actions are carefully planned with legal support.

Research & Advocacy

We conduct rigorous investigations into corporate environmental crimes, publish detailed reports, and use legal channels to hold polluters accountable. Truth is our most powerful weapon.

Funding & Transparency

Green Truth is funded entirely by individual donations. We accept no corporate money and no government funding. Our annual budget and all expenditures are published quarterly for full transparency.

In 2025, we operated on a budget of $2.3 million USD, with 87% going directly to campaign activities and chapter support. Administrative costs are kept minimal through volunteer participation.
